Our Mission

At Pawsitively Insured, our mission is to provide comprehensive and affordable pet insurance for pet owners. We are dedicated to helping pet families enjoy a worry-free life with their beloved companions by offering financial support for veterinary care and other pet-related expenses.

Our Team

Our team consists of pet lovers and industry experts who understand the unique needs of pets and their families. We are committed to providing exceptional customer service and tailored insurance solutions for our clients.

Our History

Pawsitively Insured was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate pet owners who saw the need for better insurance options in the market. Since then, we have grown into a trusted provider of pet insurance, serving thousands of happy customers and their furry friends.
